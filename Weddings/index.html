Classical Wedding Singer

 
What I Offer

On what is likely to be the most important day in your life, why not choose someone who is charming and will provide a personal and friendly service. Whether you need advice about the musical content of your service, song suggestions or someone to sing your special song, I am happy to offer you my experience and talent.

I offer a free consultation with you and your fiancé to help you select exactly the music that you would most enjoy. I have an extensive repertoire that covers everything from the traditional mass setting through to contemporary worship and modern day classics.

If there is not a church organist or another musician who you would like me to work with then I can arrange for a trusted colleague to accompany me.

Prices

Prices are flexible depending on your requirements and start from £150. Some expenses may be charged depending on the location of your wedding. Final costs will all be agreed in advance.
