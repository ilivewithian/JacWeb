Stone Choral Society

 
www.stonechoral.org.uk

Soloist

 

I have been fortunate enough to work with Charlie Penn and SCS on a number of occasions. My first performance with them was in Carmina Burana as part of their 40th anniversary celebrations. Since then I have performed Vivaldi’s Gloria and in their recent Christmas celebration; works included Mendelssohn’s Hear My Prayer.

Forthcoming work with SCS includes Mozart’s Requiem and Schubert’s Mass in G.
